Key Missing Fields to Consider

User Experience

  • • fare_brand_name - "Basic Economy" vs "Main Cabin"
  • • origin/destination_terminal - Critical for airports
  • • cabin_class_marketing_name - Airline's cabin name
  • • seat.pitch - Legroom in inches

Practical Info

  • • wifi.available - WiFi on flight
  • • power.available/type - Charging options
  • • baggage weight/dimensions - Exact limits
  • • technical stops - Hidden layovers

Transparency

  • • base_amount + tax_amount - Price breakdown
  • • total_emissions_kg - Carbon footprint
  • • available_services - Upsell options
  • • loyalty programmes - FF eligibility
